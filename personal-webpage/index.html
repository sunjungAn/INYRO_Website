ABOUT

2021 What do you do in Inyro?

INYRO, abbreviated by Brainy-Robot, intelligent robot, has 15 people interested in artificial intelligence and programming to form a group in 2005. A student in computer science who started out as a small group of programming enthusiasts, but is now promoted from various departments to a central club composed of members interested in programming.
